💪 Ultimate Gym Diet Plan 🥗

🌅 Morning (On Waking Up)

✅ Warm water with lemon OR green tea
✅ 5–6 soaked almonds

🍳 Breakfast (8–9 AM)

🥚 4 boiled egg whites + 2 whole eggs
🍞 2 slices whole wheat bread OR 1 bowl oats with milk
🍌 1 banana / seasonal fruit

🥤 Mid-Morning Snack (11 AM)

🍎 1 apple / papaya / orange
🥜 Handful of peanuts or roasted chana

🍛 Lunch (1–2 PM)

🍲 2–3 chapatis (whole wheat) OR 1 bowl rice
🥩 1 bowl dal / chicken / paneer
🥗 1 bowl seasonal vegetables
🥒 Fresh salad

⚡ Pre-Workout Snack (4–5 PM)

🍌 1 banana OR sweet potato
☕ Black coffee (no sugar) / Green tea

🏋️ Post-Workout (Immediately After)

🥤 Whey protein shake OR 4 boiled egg whites
🌴 2–3 dates / banana for quick energy

🌙 Dinner (8–9 PM)

🍗 Grilled chicken / paneer / fish
🥦 1 bowl vegetables
🌾 1–2 chapatis (skip rice at night)

🌜 Before Bed

🥛 1 glass warm milk (low-fat) OR 🥜 1 handful almonds

⚡ Pro Tips for Gym Diet

💧 Drink 3–4 liters of water daily
🚫 Avoid junk, fried, and sugary drinks
🍗 Balance protein, carbs, and healthy fats
⏳ Eat every 2.5–3 hours to keep metabolism active
